🟡 Prompt for Replit Builder

Title: Al Qadi Client Portal – Contract-Based Ordering App  

Goal:  
Build a responsive landing page for Al Qadi Co.’s Client Portal, where business clients under long-term contracts can log in, manage orders, track history, and create reusable order templates.  

---

Design & Branding
- Use the uploaded Al Qadi logo (gold olive tree on black background) as the central style reference.  
- Color palette:  
  - Gold (#d4af37 / #f9c800) for accents and buttons  
  - Deep black (#000000) for backgrounds  
  - Warm white / off-white (#fdfdfd) for text areas  
  - Subtle gray (#222222) for card backgrounds  
- Typography:  
  - Headings: Poppins Bold or Cairo Bold (for Arabic)  
  - Body: Open Sans / Noto Sans Arabic  
- Apply elegant shadows, rounded corners (2xl), and gradient gold hover effects.  
- Layout should be RTL-friendly (Arabic) and bilingual (language toggle top-right).  

---

1. Header

- Sticky, transparent header that turns solid on scroll.

  - 🟡 “تسجيل الدخول” (primary)  
  - ⚫ “طلب حساب تجريبي” (outline)
- Background: gradient black-to-gold with a subtle animation of gold particles or olive leaves.
- Side illustration: dashboard mockup (order cards, status timeline).

| Step | Title | Description |
